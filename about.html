About Us

Innovative Technology Services is a branch of Techno kids and techno future Egypt, certified from STEM.org and a partenr for CISCO Networking academy

Techno kids and techno future Kafr ElDawar branch

Since 2019 ITS (Innovative Technology Services) is a branch of Techno Kids and Techno Future Egypt.

  • Techno kids develops technology projects with lesson plans for grades 1 to 12, using a project-based approach to earning activities plans, word-processing, presentation, digital literacy, programming, web development, graphic design, and spreadsheets into curriculum.
  • Complete creative problem-solving tasks and invite students to build a webpage, publish a report, code a game, animate a story or analyze data.
  • International curricula that qualify students to excel in science, technology, engineering and programming. Training is carried out through practical workshops, models, and interesting scientific experiments in modern laboratories.
  • The project started in Canada in 1993 with more than 35 branches around the world. It started in Egypt in 2005.
  • Techno Future develops high-quality Robotics Programming, technology and engineering engaging activities to students 4-17 years to boost their future career prospects.

CISCO partner

Since 1984 Cisco helps seize the opportunities of tomorrow by proving that amazing things can happen when you connect the unconnected.

  • A comprehensive program offered by Cisco that help IT enthusiasts to enhance their skill and grasp of all things about Internetworking. They provide various heigh quality paths in IT, Networking, Programmable infrastructure, Cybersecurity and Buisness.
  • Thier mission is to train, support and foster the technical and entrepreneurial skills that people, educators, and companies require.
  • Offers quality courses to learn real career skills and pursue well-paying tech jobs.
  • Offers Digital Badges for course work that shows the skills you have acquired, industry-recognized certifications that validate competencies in a specific technology domain, and Talent Bridge program that matches you to real job opportunities through thier partner ecosystem
